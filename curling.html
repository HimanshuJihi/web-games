Welcome to the ice rink! Curling on Mlampic Games is a highly strategic, physics-based HTML5 sports mini-game. Often referred to as "chess on ice," this game challenges your precision, strategy, and sweeping skills. Play directly in your browser without any downloads.

How to Play Curling?

The rules of the game mimic the real-life Winter Olympics sport. Here is how you can dominate the ice:

  • Throwing the Stone: Click or tap on the stone, drag your finger backwards, and release to slide the stone towards the target (known as the House). The further you drag, the more power your throw will have.
  • Sweeping: While your stone is sliding, press and hold the Sweep button. Sweeping the ice reduces friction, allowing the stone to travel further.
  • Scoring: At the end of a round, only the team with the stone closest to the center (the button) scores. The winning team gets 1 point for every stone that is closer to the center than the opponent's closest stone.

Tips & Strategies to Win

Curling is not just about aiming for the center; it's about outsmarting your opponent (the blue team):

  • The Knockout (Takeout): Don't just try to score; use your stones to knock your opponent's stones out of the house!
  • Guard Stones: Place a stone just in front of the house. This acts as a shield, preventing your opponent from easily reaching the center.
  • Master the Sweep: Intentionally underpower your throw slightly. You can always use the Sweep button to add distance, but you can never slow a fast stone down!
