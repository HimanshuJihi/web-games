Play Target Practice Game Online - Free HTML5 Aiming Challenge

Test your reflexes and precision! Target Practice on Mlampic Games is a fast-paced, highly addictive HTML5 mini-game that challenges your hand-eye coordination. Can you hit all the targets before the clock runs out? Play directly in your browser with no downloads required.

How to Play Target Practice?

The rules are simple, but beating the clock is tough:

  • Shoot the Targets: Colored circular targets will randomly appear on the screen. Tap or click on them as quickly as possible to score points.
  • Beat the Clock: You only have 30 seconds per level. Make every second count!
  • Clear the Level: Reach the required target score before the timer hits zero to unlock the next, more difficult stage.

Tips and Tricks for a High Score

To master the game and complete all 5 levels, keep these strategies in mind:

  • Stay Centered: Keep your mouse pointer or finger hovering near the center of the screen to minimize travel distance to new targets.
  • Anticipate Spawns: Don't wait for the hit animation to finish. Move immediately to the next target.
