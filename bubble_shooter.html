Play Bubble Shooter Online - Free HTML5 Puzzle Game

Get ready for a timeless classic! Bubble Shooter on Mlampic Games is a fun and addictive puzzle game where the goal is to clear the board by popping colorful bubbles. It's easy to play but challenging to master. Play directly in your web browser with no downloads required.

How to Play Bubble Shooter?

The objective is to clear all the bubbles from the screen by forming groups of three or more of the same color:

  • Aim and Shoot: Use your mouse or finger to aim the shooter at the bottom. A guideline will show you the trajectory. Click or release your finger to shoot the bubble.
  • Match Colors: Your bubble will stick to the bubbles it hits. If your shot creates a group of 3 or more bubbles of the same color, they will all pop!
  • Clear the Board: Popping bubbles can cause other bubbles attached below them to fall, giving you bonus points.
  • Game Over: The game ends if the bubbles reach the bottom line of the screen.

Tips and Tricks for a High Score

To become a Bubble Shooter pro, use these strategies:

  • Bank Shots: Use the side walls to your advantage! Bouncing bubbles off the walls can help you reach tricky spots.
  • Go for High Clusters: Prioritize popping bubble clusters that are higher up. This can trigger a chain reaction, dropping all the bubbles attached below them for massive points.
  • Plan Ahead: Look at the next bubble in your shooter to plan your next one or two moves in advance.
