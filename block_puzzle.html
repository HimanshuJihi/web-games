How to Play Block Puzzle?

The goal is to score as many points as possible by clearing lines on the grid:

  • Drag and Drop: You will be given three block shapes at a time. Drag them from the bottom and place them anywhere on the 10x10 grid.
  • Clear Lines: When you complete a full horizontal or vertical line with blocks, that line will disappear, freeing up space and earning you points.
  • Get New Pieces: You must place all three given pieces on the board to receive the next set of three.
  • Game Over: The game ends when you can no longer fit any of the three available pieces onto the grid.

Scoring System

  • Placing Blocks: You get 1 point for every individual square in a block you place. (e.g., placing a 4-square block gives you 4 points).
  • Clearing Lines:
    • 1 Line: 10 Points
    • 2 Lines (Double): 30 Points
    • 3 Lines (Triple): 60 Points
    • 4 Lines (Quad): 100 Points
    • 5+ Lines (Combo): Even bigger bonus points!

Tips and Tricks for a High Score

To become a Block Puzzle master, think strategically:

  • Don't Rush: There is no time limit. Analyze the available pieces and the board before making a move.
  • Keep Space for Big Pieces: Always try to leave enough room for the large 3x3 or 5x1 blocks. Getting stuck with a big piece is the most common way to lose.
  • Aim for Combos: Clearing multiple lines at once gives a huge score bonus. Try to set up the board for doubles or triples.
  • Don't Clutter the Center: It's often easier to work from the edges inwards. A cluttered center can quickly become unplayable.
