Exercise your brain with Hoop Stack on Mlampic Games! This HTML5 color-sorting puzzle is simple to learn but difficult to master. Move the colored rings from one pole to another until each pole contains only one solid color. Play instantly in your web browser with no downloads required.

How to Play Hoop Stack?

The rules of this puzzle are straightforward, but you must think ahead:

  • Select a Ring: Tap or click on a pole to lift the top hoop.
  • Move the Ring: Tap another pole to drop the hoop there.
  • The Core Rule: You can only place a hoop on top of another hoop if they are the same color, or if the target pole is completely empty.
  • Multiple Rings: If you have multiple rings of the same color on top of a pole, they will be picked up and moved together automatically!
  • Undo Limit: You can only undo your moves 2 times per level. Use them wisely!
  • Pole Limit: A single pole can hold a maximum of 4 hoops at a time.

Tips to Solve Faster

  • Don't Rush: Unlike action games, there is no time limit. Take your time to plan your moves.
  • Free Up Empty Poles: Empty poles are your best friends. They give you the flexibility to temporarily store hoops while you organize others.
  • Work from the Bottom Up: Focus on getting the correct base colors at the bottom of the poles first.
