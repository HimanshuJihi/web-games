How to Play Zero Katta?

The rules are legendary and simple to grasp:

  • The Grid: The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Take Turns: Player 1 is 'X' and Player 2 (or the AI) is 'O'. Players take turns placing their marks in empty squares.
  • The Goal: The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) is the winner!
  • The Tie (Cat's Game): If all 9 squares are full and no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
