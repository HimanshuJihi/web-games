How to Play

1. Tap A and L (or the orange buttons) rapidly to build speed.

2. Watch the red Foul Line on the track.

3. Press JUMP (or Spacebar) right before the foul line.

⭐ Tip: Jump from the yellow zone for a PERFECT JUMP boost!

🤖 Opponent: The AI will jump after you. Try to set a hard target!

⚠️ If you cross the red line before jumping, it's a FOUL!
