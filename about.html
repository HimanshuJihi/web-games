About Us

Welcome to the Mlampic Games HTML5 Mini-Games Project!

This project is a collection of high-quality, browser-based sports and casual games designed to provide quick and engaging entertainment. From the strategic gameplay of Curling to the fast-paced action of the 100m Sprint, our goal is to bring the Olympic spirit directly to your screen.

Built using pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, these games utilize modern web technologies like the Canvas API and Web Audio API to deliver a seamless, app-like experience without the need for any downloads or external plugins.

Whether you're playing on a desktop computer or tapping away on your mobile device, our responsive design ensures you get the best possible gaming experience anywhere, anytime.

Enjoy the games and strive for the gold! 🥇
