Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you play the "Mlampic" web games.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, IP addresses, or tracking information on external servers. All gameplay is handled completely client-side in your browser.

2. Local Storage (Game Progress)

To enhance your experience, our games use your browser's built-in localStorage to save your level progress and high scores (Best Times). This data remains strictly on your device and is never sent to us or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time using the "Reset All Progress" button on the main menu.
