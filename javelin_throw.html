How to Play Javelin Throw?

Throwing the javelin perfectly requires a combination of rapid tapping and careful timing:

  • Build Speed: Tap the 'A' and 'L' buttons (or screen buttons) alternately and as rapidly as possible to make your athlete sprint down the track.
  • Aim: As you approach the red foul line, Press and Hold the 'AIM & THROW' button to set your throwing angle. Aiming for an angle close to 45° will give you the best distance.
  • Release & Throw: Let go of the button to release the javelin into the air.
  • Avoid Fouls: You must release the javelin before crossing the red line. If you cross it while holding the javelin, it will be counted as a foul and your score will be zero!

Tips and Tricks for a World Record

To beat the red-clothed AI opponent and set a new high score, keep these strategies in mind:

  • Speed is Key: The faster you run, the more initial velocity your javelin will have. Do not slow down your tapping before aiming!
  • The Golden Angle: A 45-degree angle is optimal for maximum distance. Watch the angle meter carefully and release at the exact right moment.
  • Timing the Foul Line: Do not release too early, or you will lose valuable track space. Release as close to the red line as possible without stepping over it.
