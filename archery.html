How to Play Archery?

Mastering the bow and arrow requires focus. Here are the simple rules to get you started:

  • Draw the Bow: Click/Tap and drag backwards anywhere on the screen to pull the bowstring. The further you drag, the more power your shot will have.
  • Aim: Move your finger or mouse up and down while dragging to set the perfect vertical angle.
  • Release & Shoot: Let go of the screen to release the arrow towards the target.
  • Scoring System: Hitting the Yellow center (Bullseye) gives you 10 points. The outer rings give 7 (Red), 5 (Blue), 3 (Black), and 1 (White) point respectively.

Tips and Tricks to Become a Master Archer

As you progress, the levels get harder and the target starts moving. Keep these tips in mind to clear all 5 levels:

  • Account for Gravity: Arrows do not fly in a perfectly straight line. Always aim slightly higher than the target to account for the arrow's natural drop.
  • Choose the Right Arrow: You have three types of arrows available. The Standard arrow is balanced. The Heavy arrow drops faster but is stable, and the Light arrow flies straighter but requires precise power control.
  • Time Your Shots: In later levels, the target moves up and down. Anticipate the target's position and shoot when it is about to align with your arrow's path.
