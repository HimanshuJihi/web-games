Play Carom Board Online - Free HTML5 Game

Experience the classic game of Carom Board right in your browser! Carom Board on Mlampic Games brings the fun of this traditional strike-and-pocket game to your digital device. Aim your striker, pot the colored pieces, and try to pocket the queen to win!

How to Play Carom Board?

The objective is to pocket all your assigned pieces (either white or black) before your opponent, and to pocket the red 'Queen' piece, covering it with one of your own pieces.

  • Aim: Click or tap on the striker to select it. Drag to set the aiming line and power.
  • Strike: Release to hit the striker, sending it towards the carom pieces.
  • Pocketing: If you pocket one of your pieces, you get another turn.
  • The Queen: Pocketing the red Queen gives bonus points, but you must pocket one of your own pieces immediately after (on the same or next turn) to 'cover' it. If you fail to cover, the Queen is returned to the board.
  • Fouls: Various actions can result in a foul, such as pocketing the striker, hitting the striker off the board, or failing to hit any piece. Fouls usually result in a penalty (e.g., returning a pocketed piece to the board).

Tips and Tricks for Carom Mastery

To become a Carom Board champion, consider these strategies:

  • Precision is Key: Take your time to aim. Small adjustments can make a big difference.
  • Control the Striker: Try to keep your striker on the board after a shot. Pocketing the striker is a foul.
  • Plan Your Shots: Think a few moves ahead. How will your shot affect the position of other pieces? Can you set up an easy follow-up shot?
  • Cover the Queen: Always prioritize covering the Queen once it's pocketed. It's a crucial part of scoring.
