About Us

Welcome to the Mlampic Games HTML5 Mini-Games Project!

This project is a collection of high-quality, browser-based sports and casual games designed to provide quick and engaging entertainment. From the strategic gameplay of Curling to the fast-paced action of the 100m Sprint, our goal is to bring the Olympic spirit directly to your screen.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a fun, accessible, and highly optimized gaming platform that anyone can enjoy regardless of their device. We believe in delivering instant entertainment without the hassle of app stores, long loading times, or mandatory sign-ups.

Why Choose Mlampic Games?

  • 🎮 Play Anywhere: Fully responsive design means our games work perfectly on both desktop computers and mobile phones.
  • 📶 Offline Support: Thanks to PWA technology, you can install Mlampic Games on your device and play even without an internet connection!
  • ⚡ No Downloads Required: Instantly play right in your web browser. Save your device storage.
  • 🔒 Privacy First: All your game progress, best times, and high scores are saved locally on your device. We don't track your personal gameplay data.

The Technology

We are passionate about modern web technologies. Mlampic Games is built from the ground up using pure HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript. We utilize the powerful HTML Canvas API for smooth graphics and the Web Audio API for an immersive, interactive sound experience without needing external plugins.
