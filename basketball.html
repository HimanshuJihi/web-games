Play Basketball Free Throws Online - Free HTML5 Sports Game

Step onto the court and show off your shooting skills! Basketball Free Throws on Mlampic Games is an addictive, physics-based HTML5 sports mini-game. Test your aim, swipe to shoot, and score as many baskets as you can directly in your web browser with no downloads required.

How to Play Basketball Free Throws?

The rules are straightforward, but mastering the perfect shot takes practice:

  • Shoot the Ball: Click or tap on the basketball, drag your finger or mouse upwards towards the hoop, and release to shoot. The speed and angle of your swipe determine the trajectory of the ball.
  • Beat the Clock: You have a limited amount of time (Shot Clock) for each throw. Don't take too long to aim!
  • Target Score: Each challenge requires you to make a specific number of successful shots before you run out of attempts.

Tips for the Perfect Swish

As you clear levels, the challenges will get significantly harder. Here is how you can secure the win:

  • The Moving Hoop: In later challenges, the basketball hoop will start moving left and right. Time your swipe carefully and anticipate where the hoop will be when the ball reaches its peak.
  • Consistent Swipes: Try to memorize the swipe length and speed that gives you a perfect shot from the center. Muscle memory is key!
