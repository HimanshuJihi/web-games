Play Skeet Shooting Game Online - Free HTML5 Clay Target Game

Lock and load! Skeet Shooting on Mlampic Games is a thrilling HTML5 sports mini-game that tests your reflexes, timing, and precision. Aim your crosshairs at the flying clay pigeons and blast them out of the sky before they escape. Play instantly in your web browser with no downloads required.

How to Play Skeet Shooting?

Your goal is to hit the required number of targets to clear the round, but be careful with your ammo:

  • Shoot the Clay Pigeons: Click or tap anywhere on the screen when the orange clay targets fly across the sky.
  • Watch Your Ammo: You are given a limited number of bullets per round. Don't spam your shots! Aim carefully.
  • Target Score: Every successful hit gives you 10 points. Meet the target score to progress to the next, much faster level.

Tips and Tricks for Perfect Aim

As you progress through the 5 levels, the clay targets will fly significantly faster. Here are some pro tips:

  • Lead Your Targets: Don't aim exactly where the target is; aim slightly ahead of its flight path to account for its speed.
  • Conserve Ammo: If a target is too close to the edge and about to disappear, let it go if you can afford the point loss. Wasting ammo is the easiest way to fail a level!
