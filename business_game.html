Play Business Game Online - Free HTML5 Board Game

Welcome to the ultimate property trading experience! Business Game on Mlampic Games is a digital adaptation of the classic real estate board game (similar to Monopoly). Buy properties, build houses, collect rent, and bankrupt your opponents to become the ultimate business tycoon. Play directly in your browser with no downloads required.

How to Play the Business Game?

The rules are simple but winning requires strategy and a bit of luck:

  • Roll the Dice: Click the "Roll Dice" button to move your token around the board. If you pass START, you collect ₹200.
  • Buy Properties: When you land on an unowned property, city, or utility, you can buy it. If you don't have enough money, you can choose to skip.
  • Pay Rent: If you land on a property owned by the other player, you must pay them rent based on the property's base value or current upgrades.
  • Upgrade Properties: Land on a property you already own to upgrade it! You can build up to 4 Houses (🏠) and then a Hotel (🏨). Each upgrade massively increases the rent your opponent has to pay.
  • Mortgage: Running low on cash? Click the Manage button to mortgage your properties for half their price. Mortgaged properties do not collect rent until you unmortgage them (+10% interest).
  • Chance & Community Chest: Landing on these spaces will draw a random card. You might win the lottery, get a speeding fine, or be sent directly to jail!

Game Modes and Tips

We offer two modes: Player vs Player (local multiplayer on the same screen) and Player vs Computer. If you are playing against the AI, it will automatically roll the dice and strategically buy/upgrade properties.

  • Tip: Try to buy as many properties as early as possible. Utilities (Electric Co. & Water Works) and Railroads are great for consistent early income.
  • Tip: Don't spend all your cash at once! Always keep a buffer of around ₹150-₹200 to pay unexpected rent, taxes, or fines. If your balance drops below zero, you go bankrupt and lose the game.
