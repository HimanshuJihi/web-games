How to Play Long Jump?

To achieve the perfect jump, you need to master both your running speed and your jump timing:

  • Build Speed: Tap the 'A' and 'L' buttons (or the screen buttons) alternately and rapidly. The faster you run, the further you will jump.
  • The Foul Line: Keep your eyes on the red foul line at the end of the track. You must jump before crossing it.
  • Take the Leap: Press the JUMP button (or Spacebar) to launch your athlete into the sandpit.
  • Avoid Fouls: If you step over the red line before jumping, your jump will be disqualified (FOUL) and you score 0 meters!

Tips and Tricks for a Perfect Jump

Want to beat the AI opponent and secure the gold medal? Keep these advanced strategies in mind:

  • The Yellow Zone: Just before the red foul line, there is a yellow indicator on the track. Jumping precisely from this yellow zone grants you a "Perfect Jump" boost!
  • Consistency is Key: Don't slow down your tapping as you approach the line. Maintain maximum speed until the very last millisecond.
  • Watch the AI: The computer will jump after you. Always aim for your absolute best distance so the AI can't catch up!
