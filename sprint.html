How to Play 100m Sprint?

The rules of the game are simple but mastering it requires lightning-fast fingers:

  • On Mobile/Tablet: Tap the 'A' and 'L' buttons (or the colored circular buttons) on your screen alternately and as fast as you can.
  • On Desktop/PC: Use the 'A' and 'L' keys on your keyboard to make your athlete run forward.
  • The Rule: You must alternate your taps. Pressing the same button twice in a row will not give you a speed boost. Rhythm and speed are key!

Tips and Tricks to Win the Gold Medal

Beating the AI opponent on 'Easy' is just a warm-up. If you want to conquer the 'Hard' difficulty and set a top-tier best time, follow these tips:

  • Find Your Rhythm: Don't just mash buttons blindly. Find a fast, consistent 1-2, 1-2 rhythm.
  • Use Both Hands: Place your left finger on the 'A' button and your right finger on the 'L' button to maximize tapping efficiency.
  • Perfect Start: Anticipate the "Go!" signal. Starting even a fraction of a second late can cost you the race against the Hard AI.
