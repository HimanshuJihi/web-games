How to Play

1. Tap A and L (or the orange buttons) rapidly to build speed.

2. Watch the red Foul Line.

3. PRESS AND HOLD the Aim button to set your angle. Aim for 45°!

4. RELEASE the button before the red line to throw the Javelin.

⚠️ Crossing the red line while holding the Javelin is a FOUL!
