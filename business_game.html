The Classic Business Game (Monopoly) is Coming to Mlampic!

Get ready to roll the dice, build your empire, and bankrupt your opponents! We are excited to announce that the timeless Business board game, inspired by Monopoly, is currently under development and will be joining the Mlampic Games collection soon.

What to Expect?

Our version of this classic game will feature:

  • Classic Gameplay: Buy, sell, and trade properties. Build houses and hotels to charge more rent.
  • AI Opponents: Play against smart computer opponents with varying difficulty levels.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Challenge your friends in a 'Player vs Player' mode on the same screen.
  • Modern Interface: A clean, mobile-friendly design that makes managing your properties a breeze.

Stay tuned for updates. This is going to be a big one!
